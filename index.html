Insights

Practical governance perspectives

Short advisory notes for organizations preparing for SOC 2, strengthening cybersecurity governance, or adopting AI tools.

Five SOC 2 mistakes SaaS companies make before their first audit

  1. Treating SOC 2 as documentation rather than governance.
  2. Leaving shared root, GitHub, or admin accounts in place.
  3. Starting evidence collection too late.
  4. Ignoring tenant isolation proof in multi-tenant environments.
  5. Confusing readiness support with formal audit execution.

AI governance should start before deployment

Regulated organizations adopting AI tools need a basic inventory, risk classification, human oversight model, acceptable-use expectations, and escalation process before AI becomes embedded in production workflows.
