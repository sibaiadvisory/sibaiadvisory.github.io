Information you provide is used to respond to your inquiry, assess whether there is a potential fit for an advisory engagement, schedule discussions, and maintain ordinary business correspondence.

We do not use your contact information for unrelated marketing campaigns, and we do not add you to a mailing list without your consent.

The contact form on this website is configured to open your email client and send your message to Sibai Advisory. This means your message may be processed by your own email provider and by Sibai Advisory's email service provider for transmission and delivery purposes.

We retain correspondence for a reasonable period for business record, follow-up, and engagement-management purposes. We do not sell personal information or store visitor information in advertising platforms or third-party marketing databases.

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information. We may disclose information only when necessary to respond to your request, support ordinary business operations, work with professional advisors, comply with applicable law, or respond to a valid legal request.

Depending on your location and applicable privacy law, you may have the right to request access to personal information we hold about you, request correction of inaccurate information, or withdraw consent for certain uses of your information.

To make a privacy-related request, contact mamoun@sibaiadvisory.com.
